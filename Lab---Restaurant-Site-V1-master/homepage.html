Restaurant Description
Welcome to our restaurant! We serve food that is shaped like fish, but is not actually fish. We specialize in creating fish themed desserts for people who enjoy the aquaitic aesthetic. Be able to feel like a mermaid or a cat when you order from our delicious variety of treats! Our treats include items such as: Jello molded to resemble fish, watermellon carved into the shape of a fish, fish cookies, fish shaped waffles filled with icecream, etc.
